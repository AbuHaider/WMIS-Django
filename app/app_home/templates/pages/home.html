About the WMIS

Watershed Management Information System (WMIS) of the Chittagong Hill Tracts Area aims at facilitating informed decision making at policy level for integrated management of CHT watersheds, agro-ecological landscape restoration as nature-based solutions for climate change adaptation and livelihoods improvement of indigenous communities. WMIS will further facilitate to monitor the watershed health rejuvenation and track the resilience building among indigenous communities against climate change.


About the Project

The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is committed to achieving a prosperous, inclusive, resilient, and sustainable Asia and the Pacific, while sustaining its efforts to eradicate extreme poverty. It assists its members and partners by providing loans, technical assistance, grants, and equity investments to promote social and economic development.
